# Code of Conduct

Participants are expected to keep discussion technical, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected behavior

- Be clear and specific when reporting problems.
- Treat disagreement as a normal part of technical review.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.
- Respect maintainers' decisions on project scope and release timing.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or targeted abuse.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for it to stop.
- Submitting or encouraging malicious code.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; restrict participation; or ban users from the project when necessary to keep the project usable.
